Case Study: Nike Sneaker AD - Be Explosive

Objective

Design a captivating Nike sneaker ad for print, drawing inspiration from Nike's bold marketing strategy, with a focus on invoking a powerful emotion and storytelling.

Inspiration

I sought inspiration from Nike’s advertising, mirroring their incorporation of vibrant colors, special effects, and distortion to convey a sense of movement and emotion.

Creative Process

To heighten visual impact, a screen blend mode was employed on the top flame image, and a 300 brush size was utilized for masking on the text layer, creating a burnt text effect for added visual intrigue. Furthermore, a levels adjustment enhanced vibrance by (+26) and subtly decreased saturation by (-2), resulting in a dynamic and bold aesthetic that maintained a balanced color palette.

Result

The Nike Sneaker AD, named “Be Explosive,” seamlessly blends vibrant colors, special effects, and dynamic elements. The interplay between the fire flame and sneaker tells a visually compelling story in line with Nike’s brand. The added burnt text effect provides an edgy touch, enhancing the overall impact. In essence, it captures the essence of Nike’s brand through a unique and powerful visual representation of “Be Explosive.”
